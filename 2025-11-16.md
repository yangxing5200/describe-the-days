今天去眼镜店验光了，眼镜度数左眼400，50闪光，右眼450 120闪光，然后在网上配了一副新的眼镜花了329块钱。

Today I went to the glass shop check my eyes performance, the result is left 400 right is 450, then i bought a new glass from internet cost 329 RMB.

你的英文翻译大体能表达意思，但有几处需要改善：

“glass shop” 应该是 “optical shop” 或 “eyeglasses store”；

“check my eyes performance” 不自然，应该是 “had an eye exam”；

度数要表达“近视度数 + 散光”，英文里通常说 “-4.00 with 50 astigmatism”；

“I bought a new glass” → 应该是 “a new pair of glasses”；

“cost 329 RMB” 更自然点可以是 “for 329 RMB”。

改进后的自然表达：

Today I went to an optical shop for an eye exam. My prescription is -4.00 with 50 astigmatism in the left eye, and -4.50 with 120 astigmatism in the right eye. I then ordered a new pair of glasses online for 329 RMB.

如果你想更口语化，也可以这样说：

I had my eyes checked today. My prescription came out to -4.00 with 50 astigmatism on the left and -4.50 with 120 astigmatism on the right. I bought a new pair of glasses online for 329 RMB.
