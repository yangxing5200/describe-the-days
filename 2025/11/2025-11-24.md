今天在公司开了一天的会，在做135迭代的需求评审，今天是134的最后一天，我们必须把135的需求评审结束并且把任务拆解完成。
I speet a whole day in meetting at the company, review the 135 Sprint requirements, today is lastday for 134 sprint we have to end of the 135 Sprint requirements review and create releted all of the tasks.

你的英文：

I speet a whole day in meetting at the company
问题：

speet 拼写错误 → spent

meetting 拼写错误 → meeting

at the company 不自然 → in meetings at work / at the office 更常用

“开了一天的会” → spent the whole day in meetings（注意用复数）

你的英文：

review the 135 Sprint requirements,
问题：

Sprint 一般不需要首字母都大写 → sprint

更自然的表达：reviewing the requirements for Sprint 135

你的英文：

today is lastday for 134 sprint
问题：

lastday → 两个单词 last day

for 134 sprint → Sprint 134’s last day / the last day of Sprint 134

时态也要统一

你的英文：

we have to end of the 135 Sprint requirements review and create releted all of the tasks.
问题：

end of 是名词用法，这里要动词 → finish / complete

releted → related（拼写错误）

“把任务拆解完成” → break down the tasks / create all related tasks

语序需要优化

要用复数 tasks
Today I spent the whole day in meetings at work, reviewing the requirements for Sprint 135. Since today is last day of Sprint 134, we had to finished the requirements review for Sprint 135 and break down all the related tasks.
